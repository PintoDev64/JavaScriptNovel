# Code of Conduct

As contributors and maintainers of **NovelJs**, we are committed to fostering an inclusive, respectful, and collaborative community. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for participants and the consequences of unacceptable behavior.

## Our Standards

To maintain a positive and productive environment, we expect all participants to:

- **Be Respectful**: Treat others with respect, regardless of background, experience, or opinions.
- **Collaborate Openly**: Share knowledge, offer constructive feedback, and welcome contributions from others.
- **Focus on the Project**: Keep discussions and contributions relevant to **NovelJs**.
- **Respect Boundaries**: Avoid personal attacks, harassment, or any behavior that might make others uncomfortable.
- **Be Patient**: Understand that everyone has different levels of experience and that mistakes are part of the learning process.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following actions are not tolerated:

- Harassment or discrimination of any kind.
- Offensive comments or content, including those related to race, gender, sexual orientation, or religion.
- Demeaning or insulting language.
- Disruptive behavior that hinders collaboration or the progress of the project.

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness any behavior that violates this Code of Conduct:

1. Contact the maintainer directly at **[joan.pinto.dev@gmail.com]**.
2. Provide as much detail as possible, including dates, participants, and any supporting evidence.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated confidentially.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct. Participants who violate the rules may face the following consequences, depending on the severity:

- A private warning.
- Temporary or permanent ban from the project’s GitHub repository.
- Other actions as deemed necessary to protect the community.
